Ethical & Legal Boundaries

SignalScan is designed to provide insight into your public digital surface without crossing legal or ethical lines. We respect the security and privacy of our clients and the public, and we operate within the following boundaries:

What We Do

  • Perform ordinary web browsing and search‑engine research.
  • Inspect public DNS records and certificate transparency logs.
  • Observe TLS certificates and HTTP response headers using HEAD or GET requests.
  • Review public business directories and social‑media profiles.
  • Analyse the customer journey from a mobile perspective without submitting real data.
  • Identify publicly available privacy notices, cookie banners and terms.
  • Provide opinions on trust, exposure and missed opportunities based on public information.

What We Do Not Do

  • Perform penetration testing or vulnerability exploitation.
  • Guess or brute‑force credentials or bypass authentication.
  • Run intrusive scanners, port scans or directory enumeration.
  • Submit false contact or booking forms that could trigger business workflows.
  • Access private data or systems without authorisation.
  • Scrape websites in breach of terms or use automated tools beyond respectful, passive checks.
  • Claim compliance, certification, guaranteed leads or revenue.

Scope and Consent

We operate only on domains and public profiles you explicitly approve. You may withdraw or add assets at any time. We will not probe assets outside of the agreed scope.
