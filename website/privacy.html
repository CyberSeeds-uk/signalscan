Privacy Notice

This privacy notice explains how Jxnesyy Signal Lab collects and uses personal data in connection with the SignalScan service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with relevant data‑protection law.

What We Collect

During initial contact we may collect your name, business name, email address, telephone number and the URL of your primary website. When you purchase SignalScan we collect payment status and scope details. We do not collect or store customer personal data, login credentials or sensitive information about your employees or clients.

How We Use It

Your information is used solely to deliver the SignalScan service, communicate with you about your report and manage our relationship. We will not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g. payment processors or form providers) who process data on our behalf under appropriate contracts.

Legal Basis

We process your data on the basis of legitimate interests in providing professional services and, when you purchase a service, on the basis of contract. You may withdraw your interest or request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Retention

We keep records of engagements and reports for as long as necessary to fulfil our contract and as required for financial or legal obligations, typically not exceeding twelve months. Evidence collected about your public digital surface is anonymised or deleted after the engagement unless otherwise agreed.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including access controls, encryption in transit and secure deletion. Our passive data collection avoids ingesting unnecessary personal data.
