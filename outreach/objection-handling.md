# Objection Handling Guide

When prospects raise objections, respond respectfully and concisely.  Below are common scenarios and suggested responses.

## “Not interested”

“Thanks for letting me know.  I appreciate your time and will not contact you again.  If you ever decide you’d like an external perspective on your digital trust signals, feel free to reach out.”

## “How much?”

“The full SignalScan is £750, but we currently offer a founding price of £295 to the first five qualifying clients in exchange for structured feedback.  The Three‑Signal Preview is complimentary so you can see the value before committing.”

## “We already have someone”

“That’s great.  SignalScan complements, rather than replaces, existing marketing or security efforts by showing what customers and competitors can already see.  It doesn’t require any access to your systems.  If you’d like an independent perspective, let me know.”

## “Send information”

“Absolutely.  I can send you a brief overview and a sample Three‑Signal Preview.  What’s the best email address to use?”

## “Is this a security test?”

“SignalScan is a passive assessment.  We do not perform penetration testing, exploit vulnerabilities or access private data.  We review only publicly visible information to identify trust signals, gaps and friction.  Think of it as a ‘digital first impression’ audit.”

## “Can you guarantee results?”

“We guarantee that your report will contain at least five actionable findings or your fee is refunded.  We cannot promise specific revenue, rankings or leads because many factors influence those outcomes.  Our role is to provide evidence and recommendations.”
