# Follow‑Up Sequence

Respect your prospects’ time.  Send no more than two follow‑up messages after the initial outreach, and always provide a clear opt‑out.

## Follow‑Up 1 (2–3 business days later)

Subject: Re: Three‑Signal Preview for [Prospect Business]

Hi [Name],

Just checking in on my offer to prepare a short Three‑Signal Preview for [Prospect Business].  It would highlight one positive trust signal, one gap and one friction point in your digital journey, all based on public information.

If you’re not interested, please let me know and I won’t contact you again.  If you’d like to see the preview, simply reply “yes” and I’ll get started.

Thanks again,

Nathan

## Follow‑Up 2 (7 business days later)

Subject: Final note from Jxnesyy Signal Lab

Hi [Name],

I haven’t heard back about the Three‑Signal Preview, so this will be my last message unless you reply.  If you ever want an external perspective on how your online presence affects trust and enquiries, feel free to reach out.

Wishing you the best with [Prospect Business].

Nathan

---

If the prospect replies “not interested”, thank them and remove them from your outreach list.  Do not send further messages.  Always respect privacy and do not include sensitive findings in unsolicited emails.
