# Video Audit Script

Use this script to record a short screencast walking a prospect through their Three‑Signal Preview.  Keep the video under five minutes and focus on clarity and encouragement.

1. **Introduction (30 seconds)**
   - Introduce yourself and Jxnesyy Signal Lab.
   - Explain the purpose of the video: to walk through their Three‑Signal Preview.

2. **Positive Observation (60 seconds)**
   - Show the page or element where the business is doing well (e.g. valid TLS certificate, recent reviews).
   - Explain why this matters for trust.

3. **Verified Trust Gap (60 seconds)**
   - Navigate to the page with the trust gap (e.g. broken privacy notice link).
   - Demonstrate the issue and explain its potential impact.

4. **Customer‑Journey Obstruction (60 seconds)**
   - On a mobile emulator or device, show the friction point (e.g. confusing booking date picker).
   - Describe how this might cause drop‑offs.

5. **Missed Opportunity (30 seconds)**
   - Briefly mention an opportunity not yet taken (e.g. featuring positive reviews).

6. **Next Steps (30 seconds)**
   - Explain that the full SignalScan delivers a comprehensive report with a priority matrix, 24‑hour actions and a 30‑day roadmap.
   - Mention the founding price and the guarantee.
   - Invite them to get in touch if they’d like to proceed.

Ensure your tone is friendly and constructive.  Avoid using technical jargon or implying that the business is failing.  Highlight progress as much as gaps.
