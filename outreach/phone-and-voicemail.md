# Phone and Voicemail Script

## Phone Call

“Hi [Name], my name is Nathan Jones from Jxnesyy Signal Lab.  I know you’re busy, so I’ll be brief.  I specialise in helping independent garages like [Prospect Business] understand what customers and competitors can see about them online.

I was looking at your site and noticed [specific, non‑alarmist observation].  I offer a complimentary Three‑Signal Preview that highlights one positive signal, one trust gap and one customer‑journey friction point.  It’s based solely on public information – no intrusive scans.

Would you be interested in receiving it?  If so, I can send it via email or whichever channel you prefer.”

– If they agree: “Great, what’s the best email to send it to?  You’ll receive it within a couple of days.”
– If they decline: “No worries at all.  Thanks for your time and have a great day.”
