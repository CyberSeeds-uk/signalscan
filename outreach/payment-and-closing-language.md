# Payment and Closing Language

Use this language when the prospect agrees to purchase the full SignalScan.  Make payment terms clear, reaffirm the value and set expectations.

## Closing Email

Subject: Getting Started with SignalScan

Hi [Name],

I’m excited to work with you on the SignalScan for [Business].  To confirm, the founding price is £295 (standard price £750).  This price is offered in exchange for structured feedback and permission to request a testimonial – you are under no obligation to give a positive testimonial.

Please use the secure payment link below to complete the purchase:

[PAYMENT LINK]

Once payment has cleared, I will send you a short intake form to confirm scope and approved assets.  We will then begin the assessment and deliver your report within 48 hours.

If you have any questions, feel free to reply to this email or call me on [Your Phone].

Thanks again and speak soon,

Nathan

## Payment Link

Replace `[PAYMENT LINK]` with the URL specified in `PAYMENT_LINK_URL` in your configuration.  Ensure the link directs to a secure checkout page hosted by your payment processor.  Do not embed payment forms directly in emails.
