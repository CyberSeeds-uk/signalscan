# Pre‑Engagement Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure all necessary information and approvals are in place before starting a SignalScan engagement.

1. **Client Contact Details**
   - [ ] Full name and role of the authorised client representative
   - [ ] Email address and telephone number

2. **Scope Definition**
   - [ ] Primary domain confirmed
   - [ ] Additional domains or subdomains approved
   - [ ] Public profiles and directories approved
   - [ ] Three competitor URLs supplied
   - [ ] Any exclusions or special considerations noted

3. **Consent and Authority**
   - [ ] Client confirms they have authority to engage Jxnesyy Signal Lab
   - [ ] Client accepts the Service Scope and Engagement Terms
   - [ ] Consent captured via signature or electronic acceptance

4. **Payment**
   - [ ] Invoice issued or payment link sent
   - [ ] Payment received and cleared

5. **Scheduling**
   - [ ] 48‑hour delivery window agreed
   - [ ] Client contact available for clarification during assessment

6. **Configuration**
   - [ ] YAML configuration file prepared for passive collector (see `tools/config.example.yaml`)
   - [ ] Respectful delay and user agent set

7. **Onboarding Communication**
   - [ ] Welcome email sent with confirmation of start time and expectations
   - [ ] Client directed to privacy notice and terms

Completing this checklist reduces misunderstandings and ensures a smooth assessment.
