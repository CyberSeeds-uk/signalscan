# Data Retention Policy

This policy outlines how Jxnesyy Signal Lab stores and disposes of data collected during the delivery of the SignalScan service.

## Purpose

To ensure that personal data, business information and evidence collected during engagements are retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil contractual, legal and operational requirements, and are securely deleted thereafter.

## Principles

- **Minimal Collection** – Only collect data that is required to perform the service.
- **Defined Retention Periods** – Establish clear retention periods for different categories of data.
- **Secure Storage** – Store data securely with appropriate access controls and encryption where practical.
- **Secure Deletion** – When the retention period expires, delete or anonymise data in a manner that prevents recovery.

## Retention Periods

| Data Category | Retention Period | Disposal Method |
|---------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Contact and engagement details | 12 months after engagement completion | Permanently delete from CRM and email systems |
| Payment records | 6 years | Retain in accordance with financial regulations; securely archive |
| Evidence collected (screenshots, HTTP headers, public data) | 6 months after report delivery | Securely delete or anonymise |
| Reports and findings | 3 years | Archive for client access and internal reference; delete upon client request |
| Feedback forms | 12 months | Aggregate insights; delete individual responses |

## Responsibilities

The founder, Nathan Jones, is responsible for implementing this policy.  All team members must follow data retention procedures and report any issues immediately.

## Review

This policy shall be reviewed annually or whenever there is a significant change in processing activities or legal requirements.
