The hidden problem

Your business is being judged before anyone calls.

Customers make fast trust decisions using whatever your public digital surface gives them. Most businesses never see the full picture those visitors see.

Trust leakage

Small inconsistencies create large doubts.

Broken policy links, outdated information, mismatched opening hours and weak proof signals can quietly undermine confidence.

Journey friction

Interested customers meet preventable obstacles.

Poor mobile layouts, confusing forms, dead-end buttons and unclear calls to action make contacting you harder than it should be.

Public exposure

Your visible footprint may reveal more than intended.

Old pages, forgotten profiles, exposed documents and avoidable technical signals can create reputation and security concerns.

Missed leverage

Your strongest proof may be sitting unused.

Reviews, specialist capabilities, local reputation and customer language often exist publicly but are not converted into trust.

Questions

Clear boundaries before work begins.

Is SignalScan a penetration test?

No. SignalScan is a passive external digital trust assessment. It does not attempt to exploit vulnerabilities, gain access, bypass controls or interact with private systems.

What does the free Three-Signal Preview include?

It contains three concise, publicly observable signals selected to demonstrate the type of evidence SignalScan can surface. It is not a substitute for the complete assessment.

Will you fix the issues you identify?

The core service is the intelligence report and roadmap. Separately scoped implementation support may be available after delivery, depending on the nature of the work required.

Do you guarantee leads, revenue or higher rankings?

No. SignalScan guarantees a minimum level of evidence-backed, actionable findings under the stated refund policy. Commercial, search and revenue outcomes depend on many factors outside the assessment.

What information do you collect?

The preview form collects only the details needed to understand the business and respond. The assessment itself is focused on publicly available business information and the agreed public digital surface.

Can this be used outside the automotive sector?

The founding offer is intentionally focused on garages, MOT centres and automotive specialists so the method can be validated consistently. Other high-trust local service sectors may be considered later.
