# Structured Feedback Form

Clients who purchase the Founding SignalScan at the discounted price agree to provide structured feedback.  Use this form to capture useful insights without pressuring them for a positive testimonial.  Suggested sections:

1. **Overall Experience** – Likert scale or rating on the clarity, usefulness and professionalism of the service.
2. **Most Valuable Sections** – Multiple choice or open text asking which report sections were most actionable.
3. **Areas for Improvement** – Open text inviting constructive feedback on communication, report layout, technical depth or any other aspect.
4. **Unexpected Insights** – Ask what surprised the client or what they would not have discovered on their own.
5. **Follow‑Up Interest** – Gauge interest in SignalRepair or SignalWatch.
6. **Testimonial Permission** – Checkbox to indicate whether the client consents to be contacted for a testimonial.  Make clear that a positive testimonial is not required and that permission may be withdrawn.

Use the feedback to refine your process and materials.  Do not publish comments without explicit permission.
