# Seven‑Day Revenue Sprint

This sprint plan is designed to generate revenue within the first week of launching SignalScan.  It combines targeted outreach, content marketing and operational discipline.

## Day 1: Final Prep & Announcements

1. Complete the launch day runbook and ensure the website and forms are live.
2. Post an announcement on LinkedIn and Instagram introducing SignalScan and the founding offer.  Use an engaging image and link to the site.
3. Send a personal message to your existing network (friends, ex‑colleagues) explaining the new venture and asking for referrals.

## Day 2: Prospect Research

1. Identify at least 30 local garages, MOT centres and automotive specialists in Greater Manchester.
2. Use the research checklist to gather data and score each prospect.
3. Populate the prospect tracker and prioritise the top 10 prospects.

## Day 3: Targeted Outreach

1. Send personalised cold emails to the top 10 prospects using the scripts provided.
2. Send LinkedIn connection requests to decision‑makers at those businesses with a short introductory note.
3. Leave voicemails for hard‑to‑reach contacts if phone numbers are available.

## Day 4: Follow‑Up

1. Monitor responses from Day 3 and schedule calls or prepare Three‑Signal Previews as requested.
2. Send first follow‑up messages to prospects who did not respond.
3. Record all contact activity in the prospect tracker.

## Day 5: Content & Authority

1. Publish a LinkedIn article or blog post explaining the importance of external digital trust intelligence for garages, including anonymised examples from the sample report.
2. Share the post with relevant local groups and hashtags.
3. Reach out to local trade associations or business networks to explore guest posts or webinar opportunities.

## Day 6: Deliver Previews

1. Finalise and send any requested Three‑Signal Previews.
2. Offer a brief call to discuss the findings and explain the full SignalScan.
3. Close deals with those ready to proceed; send payment and intake forms.

## Day 7: Review & Adjust

1. Evaluate outreach effectiveness: open rates, responses, conversions.
2. Identify any objections or barriers and refine scripts accordingly.
3. Plan the next week based on lessons learned, adjusting the prospect list and messaging.

Staying focused and consistent during this sprint will maximise the chances of securing your first paying clients quickly.
