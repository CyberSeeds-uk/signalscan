Executive Summary

Example Garage operates a functional website but misses key trust signals and suffers from friction in its mobile booking journey. A broken privacy notice, missing DMARC record and confusing date picker stand out as high‑priority issues. Competitors load faster and showcase certifications. Positive aspects include a valid TLS certificate and enthusiastic customer reviews that could be leveraged.

Key Findings

Surface

  • Unoptimised hero images: Large homepage images slow down mobile load times. Compress images and use modern formats.

Integrity

  • Missing DMARC record: Add a DMARC policy to reduce domain spoofing risk.

Gateway

  • Confusing date picker: Simplify the booking form’s date and time selector to reduce abandonment.

Narrative

  • Underused five‑star reviews: Highlight genuine customer praise on the website to build trust.

Advantage

  • Competitor speed advantage: Competitors load booking pages in under a second and feature clear calls to action. Optimise your own page speed.

Leverage

  • Privacy notice 404: Restore and link to the privacy notice to comply with expectations and policies.

24‑Hour Actions

  • Publish a privacy notice and fix the footer link.
  • Compress homepage images to under 200 KB.
  • Plan a redesign of the booking date picker for mobile usability.

30‑Day Roadmap

  • Add a DMARC record with appropriate policy.
  • Integrate genuine testimonials and star ratings.
  • Optimise overall page speed and study competitor practices.
