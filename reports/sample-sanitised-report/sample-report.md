# SignalScan Sample Report – Example Garage

**Date:** 9 June 2026  
**Confidentiality:** This document is fictitious and provided for demonstration purposes only.

## Executive Summary

Example Garage has a respectable online presence but lacks certain trust signals and suffers from usability issues on mobile.  Fixing a broken privacy notice link, implementing a DMARC record and simplifying the booking form will improve trust and increase bookings.  Competitors perform better on page speed and call‑to‑action clarity.  Positive reviews present an opportunity to enhance credibility.

## Key Findings

### S — Surface

- **Unoptimised hero images (SG‑S‑001):** Large homepage images (2.5 MB) slow down mobile load times.  Compress images and use modern formats.

### I — Integrity

- **Missing DMARC record (SG‑I‑001):** No DMARC policy is configured.  Add a DMARC record to reduce domain spoofing risk.

### G — Gateway

- **Confusing date picker (SG‑G‑001):** The booking form’s date picker is unintuitive on mobile.  Simplify the widget to reduce abandonment.

### N — Narrative

- **Underused five‑star reviews (SG‑N‑001):** Positive customer reviews are not featured on the site.  Highlight genuine testimonials to build trust.

### A — Advantage

- **Competitor speed advantage (SG‑A‑001):** Competitors load booking pages faster and highlight clear calls to action.  Improve page speed and clarity to stay competitive.

### L — Leverage

- **Privacy notice 404 (SG‑L‑001):** The privacy notice link returns a 404 page.  Publish a privacy notice and update the footer link.
