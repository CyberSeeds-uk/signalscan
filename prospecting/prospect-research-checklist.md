# Prospect Research Checklist

Use this checklist when researching new prospects in the automotive sector.  It helps determine whether the business is a good fit for SignalScan and what initial signals might be worth mentioning in outreach.

1. **Basic Information**
   - [ ] Business name and trading style
   - [ ] Location and service area
   - [ ] Number of employees (approximate)
   - [ ] Contact details (phone, email, contact form)

2. **Digital Presence**
   - [ ] Primary website URL and any subdomains
   - [ ] Presence on Google Business, Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn
   - [ ] Last update date on social profiles
   - [ ] Presence on review platforms (Google, TrustPilot, Yell, etc.)

3. **Trust Signals**
   - [ ] Visible testimonials or reviews on the website
   - [ ] Certifications, memberships or guarantees shown
   - [ ] Clear privacy notice and terms
   - [ ] Secure (HTTPS) with valid certificate

4. **Customer Journey**
   - [ ] Primary call to action (phone call, booking form, quote form)
   - [ ] Ease of use on mobile (no horizontal scrolling, legible fonts)
   - [ ] Friction points (long forms, confusing navigation, broken links)

5. **Competitive Landscape**
   - [ ] Identify at least three local competitors
   - [ ] Compare trust signals and booking journeys
   - [ ] Note any standout differentiators (e.g. awards, specialism)

6. **Scoring**
   - [ ] Assign points using the scorecard in `prospect-scorecard.csv`
   - [ ] Determine the band (Priority, Strong, Contact Only, Ignore)
   - [ ] Record the score and band in `prospect-tracker.csv`

7. **Sensitive Considerations**
   - [ ] Avoid collecting personal data about individuals
   - [ ] Do not access private systems or attempt to book services
   - [ ] Respect privacy and do not store unnecessary information

Completing this checklist ensures a consistent, ethical and data‑driven approach to prospecting.
