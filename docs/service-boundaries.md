# Service Boundaries

SignalScan operates at the intersection of cybersecurity, customer‑experience analysis and competitive intelligence. To maintain trust and comply with ethical and legal obligations, the service is deliberately scoped. The boundaries below describe what SignalScan does and does **not** do.

## What We Do

- **Passive Observation:** All technical checks rely on publicly available information such as DNS records, TLS certificates, HTTP responses, public reviews and visible content. No credentials or authentication are ever used.
- **Mobile Journey Testing:** Analysts interact with websites as a normal visitor would, primarily on mobile, to evaluate clarity, friction and accessibility without submitting real personal data.
- **Reputational Analysis:** Public reviews, business‑profile content and social‑media presence are reviewed for themes, consistency and trust gaps. Personal data of reviewers is not republished.
- **Competitor Comparison:** Three relevant competitors are assessed using the same passive criteria to identify relative strengths and opportunities. Comparisons avoid speculation about private metrics like traffic or revenue.
- **Prioritisation and Roadmapping:** Findings are scored individually on multiple dimensions and converted into actionable plans with clear ownership and timelines.
- **Clear Reporting:** Every observation is tied to a source, timestamp and confidence level. Distinctions are made between fact, interpretation and recommendation, and limitations are stated.

## What We Do Not Do

- **Penetration Testing:** SignalScan is not a penetration test. It does not exploit, verify or demonstrate vulnerabilities through active intrusion.
- **Intrusive Scanning:** Techniques such as port scanning, brute forcing, path fuzzing and vulnerability scanning tools (e.g. nmap, masscan, ffuf, nuclei) are not used.
- **Authentication or Access Control Bypass:** Analysts do not attempt to log in to systems, guess credentials or circumvent any access controls.
- **Form Submission or Workflow Triggering:** Unless explicitly approved by the client, enquiry, quote or booking forms are not submitted and no automated emails or workflows are triggered.
- **Private Data Collection:** The service does not seek or store sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary for contact and billing. Reviewer identities are not exposed.
- **Guarantees of Revenue or Compliance:** SignalScan does not promise leads, rankings, conversions, regulatory compliance or certification. It provides evidence‑backed insights to inform decisions.

## Client Responsibilities

Clients are expected to:

- Provide accurate information about approved domains, public profiles and competitors.
- Review and approve the engagement scope prior to collection.
- Implement recommendations within their own legal and regulatory context.
- Seek specialist legal or technical advice where required. SignalScan is not a substitute for professional legal, cybersecurity or accessibility assessments.

By adhering to these boundaries, SignalScan delivers practical, trusted intelligence without overstepping ethical lines or creating unnecessary risk.
