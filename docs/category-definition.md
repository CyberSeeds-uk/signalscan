# External Digital Trust Intelligence

**External Digital Trust Intelligence (EDTI)** is a new service category created by Jxnesyy Signal Lab.  It focuses on illuminating the public digital surface of a business to help founders build trust, reduce risk and convert more enquiries.

## What It Is

EDTI combines elements of cybersecurity, digital anthropology, customer journey analysis and competitive intelligence.  Rather than performing deep technical penetration testing or promising search‑engine rankings, EDTI looks at what customers, competitors and opportunistic attackers can already see:

- Public websites, subdomains and business profiles
- Technical trust indicators such as TLS certificates and email authentication records
- Visible policies, reviews and reputational signals
- Mobile booking and enquiry journeys
- Comparisons with competitors’ public offerings

## Why It Matters

For founder‑led service businesses, the first digital impression can make or break a potential enquiry.  An outdated site, broken forms or missing privacy notice signals a lack of care and may deter customers.  Conversely, clear calls to action and visible trust cues build confidence.  EDTI provides a structured, ethical and actionable way to understand and improve these signals without intruding into internal systems.

## How It Differs

- **Not Penetration Testing:** EDTI is passive and does not exploit or verify vulnerabilities through intrusion.  It complements, rather than replaces, technical security testing.
- **Not SEO or Marketing Audit:** While customer journey and messaging are considered, EDTI does not guarantee rankings or leads.  It focuses on trust and friction.
- **Evidence‑Backed:** Findings are tied to specific observations and include interpretations and recommendations with confidence levels.

EDTI empowers founders to take control of their public digital presence and prioritise improvements with clear guidance.
