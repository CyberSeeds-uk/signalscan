# Operating Metrics

SignalScan is a service business. To maintain quality, improve processes and demonstrate value, a small set of operating metrics are tracked internally. These metrics inform continuous improvement but are not shared publicly without context.

## Service Quality

- **Average Findings per Report:** Tracks the typical number of verified observations delivered to clients. Used to ensure reports are comprehensive but not padded with low‑impact items.
- **Finding Distribution:** Measures the percentage of findings across the SIGNAL stages (Surface, Integrity, Gateway, Narrative, Advantage, Leverage). An imbalance may highlight areas for tooling or training improvements.
- **Client Feedback Score:** Aggregated from structured feedback forms completed after each engagement. Scores cover clarity of report, perceived value, ease of understanding and likelihood to recommend.
- **Revision Rate:** Percentage of reports requiring significant factual corrections. A low rate indicates strong accuracy and internal review processes.

## Timeliness

- **Mean Time to Deliver:** Average time from scope confirmation to delivery of the final report. The target for the 48‑hour SignalScan is two business days.
- **On‑Time Delivery Rate:** Percentage of engagements delivered within the agreed timeframe. Delays trigger a review of resource allocation and process steps.

## Operational Efficiency

- **Passive Check Coverage:** Tracks which passive checks were enabled and executed across engagements, ensuring consistent application of the toolset.
- **Tool Run Duration:** Monitors how long the collection script runs for different domains. Outliers can indicate network issues or scope creep.
- **Automation Ratio:** Measures the portion of observations generated via automated tooling versus manual analyst input. A healthy balance ensures efficiency without sacrificing human judgement.

## Business Performance

- **Conversion Rate from Preview to Full Scan:** Percentage of Three‑Signal Preview recipients who purchase the full SignalScan. Helps assess the effectiveness of the preview and sales scripts.
- **Client Retention:** Tracks repeat purchases or subscription upgrades (e.g. to SignalWatch) over time.
- **Average Realised Price:** Compares founding offer uptake to standard pricing to monitor discount programmes and ensure profitability.

These metrics are reviewed during regular retrospectives and feed into updates to tooling, processes and training. They are not used to create simplistic scores in client reports; instead, they guide internal decision‑making.
