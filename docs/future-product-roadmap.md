# Future Product Roadmap

SignalScan is the first offering from Jxnesyy Signal Lab. The roadmap below outlines possible evolutions of the service. These ideas are exploratory and subject to client demand, ethical considerations and regulatory compliance. They do not constitute guarantees or public commitments.

## SignalWatch – Continuous Monitoring

Transition the one‑off SignalScan into a subscription that monitors clients’ public digital estate on an ongoing basis.  Key features may include:

- Scheduled passive checks for DNS, TLS, SPF/DMARC and HTTP headers with change alerts.
- Automated notification when new pages, subdomains or third‑party scripts appear.
- Integration with review platforms to surface new customer feedback and sentiment shifts.
- Quarterly mini‑reports highlighting trend lines rather than duplicating the full SignalScan.

## SignalRepair – Implementation Support

Offer hands‑on assistance in remediating findings, such as drafting privacy notices, improving mobile navigation or optimising call‑to‑action flows.  This would be delivered under separate terms, potentially in collaboration with trusted partners for legal or technical tasks.

## Platform Automation

Where appropriate, automate labour‑intensive tasks while maintaining human oversight. Examples include:

- A web dashboard for clients to view their surface map and findings over time.
- Automated integration with domain and certificate transparency APIs to surface changes.
- Template generators for policies and disclosure processes based on best practice.

## Expanded Vertical Coverage

While the initial focus is on founder‑led automotive service businesses in Greater Manchester, the methodologies can adapt to other sectors with high trust requirements. Future verticals might include:

- Professional services (accountants, legal advisors, architects)
- Healthcare providers (private clinics, dentists) with appropriate privacy frameworks
- Regional e‑commerce businesses where customer trust drives conversion

## Training and Accreditation

Develop an internal training curriculum and, if appropriate, a practitioner manual or accreditation for external consultants.  Any accreditation would prioritise ethical practice and could include continuing‑education requirements and peer review.

## Research and Thought Leadership

Publish anonymised, aggregated insights on the state of external digital trust across different sectors.  This could include trend reports, benchmark studies and contributions to developing standards in domestic cyber ecology.

**Note:** All future products will undergo the same rigorous ethical review as SignalScan. Nothing in this roadmap implies expansion into penetration testing, intrusive scanning or any activity that contradicts the service boundaries outlined in this kit.
